Lulyboo Lovey Baby Sleep Soother

A perfect blend of comfort and technology, the Lulyboo Lovey Baby Sleep Soother is designed to help your little one drift into a peaceful, restful sleep.

Safe & Durable for Baby

Crafted in the USA, this sleep soother is built with your baby's safety as a top priority. Made from soft, baby-friendly materials and BPA-free components, it's gentle on sensitive skin and tough enough to handle daily use. The reinforced design ensures long-lasting comfort, making it an ideal companion for your baby's bedtime routine.

Convenient Built-In Timer

With a built-in timer, the Lulyboo sleep soother allows you to set automatic shutoff options at 5, 15, or 30 minutes. This not only helps conserve battery life but also ensures your baby gets just the right amount of soothing without the hassle of manual adjustments.

Soothing Sound & White Noise Options

Choose from a variety of calming sound options to create a peaceful sleep environment. The Lulyboo soother features 3 soothing melodies — Brahms, Rockabye Baby, and Twinkle — along with 3 white noise selections — Heartbeat, Womb Sound, and Waves. These sounds are designed to comfort your baby and promote a calm, restful night's sleep.

Customizable Vibration Settings

This sleep soother offers adjustable vibration levels — soft, medium, and strong — to cater to your baby's unique needs. The removable sound module makes it easy to clean and maintain, while the device comes fully assembled with 2 AA batteries included for instant use.

Universal Leg Loops for Easy Attach

The universal leg loops allow for effortless attachment and detachment, making it easy to use in any crib or sleep space. Whether you're on the go or at home, this feature ensures convenience and flexibility without disrupting your baby's sleep.

The Lulyboo Lovey Baby Sleep Soother is more than just a sleep aid — it's a comforting presence that helps your baby feel secure and relaxed. From the moment it's placed in their crib, it offers a blend of sound, vibration, and soft touch, creating a safe and soothing environment for better sleep and happier moments.
