Musical Crib Mobile with 12 Lullabies

Bring a touch of soothing serenity to your baby’s sleep space with the Petino Pura Musical Crib Mobile. Designed with baby-safe materials and a calming melody feature, this mobile supports early development and helps create a tranquil environment for your little one.

Calming Melodies for Peaceful Sleep

With a built-in speaker that plays 12 gentle lullabies, this crib mobile offers a soothing soundscape to help your baby relax and fall asleep more easily. The adjustable volume ensures you can tailor the audio to your baby's comfort, whether it’s a quiet bedtime or a lively playtime.

Engaging Plush Toys for Sensory Development

Featuring 4 soft animal plush toys and a heart, this mobile encourages visual tracking and hand-eye coordination. The detachable design allows easy access for play or cleaning, making it a versatile and safe addition to your baby’s nursery décor.

Elegant Design & Easy Assembly

Simple to install and made for standard cribs, this mobile comes with an easy-to-follow manual to ensure quick setup. The rotating arm adds an extra touch of charm, and the automatic shut-off after 30 minutes provides peace of mind for parents.

Safe & Durable Materials

Constructed from high-quality, baby-safe materials such as soft polyester and durable plastic, this mobile meets strict safety standards. It's the perfect choice for parents who want both style and security in their baby’s room.

A Gentle Reminder: Battery Required

This musical crib mobile requires 2 AA batteries (not included) to operate. It's designed for easy use and can be assembled in minutes, bringing immediate comfort and calm to your baby’s sleep environment.

Enhance your baby's sensory experience and create a peaceful bedtime routine with the Petino Pura Musical Crib Mobile. A thoughtful combination of music, design, and safety makes this the ideal décor piece for your nursery.
