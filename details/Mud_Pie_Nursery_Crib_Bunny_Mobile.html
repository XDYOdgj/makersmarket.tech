Mud Pie Nursery Crib Bunny Mobile, White

This charming hand-crafted crib mobile is designed to bring warmth and whimsy to your baby's nursery. Made from soft, durable boiled wool, the gentle bunnies are carefully arranged in a cascading design, adding a touch of subtle elegance and comfort to any room.

The mobile features a wooden hoop with acrylic yarn pom-poms and tassels, creating a delightful visual and sensory experience for your baby. It's securely hung with a cotton cord loop, ensuring safety and a secure installation.

Its size of 32 inches by 10 inches makes it the perfect centerpiece for your baby's crib or nursery decor. Whether you're looking for a peaceful addition to your baby's space or a stylish piece to complement your nursery theme, this mobile is a thoughtful and elegant choice.

Always ensure the mobile is hung out of your baby's reach for safe use.
