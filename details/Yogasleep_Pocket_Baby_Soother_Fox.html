Yogasleep Pocket Baby Soother Fox

A Portable White Noise Machine Designed for Peaceful Sleep

Trusted for Over 60 Years

Since 1962, Yogasleep has been a leader in creating calming white noise for babies. The Pocket Baby Soother brings the same signature sound to your child, offering a unique blend of rushing air and gentle melodies to help them drift off and stay asleep. It’s perfect for any nursery or bedtime routine.

6 Calming Sleep Sounds & Timer

Choose from six professionally curated sounds such as white noise, brown noise, our signature Dohm fan sound, soft waves, Twinkle-Twinkle Little Star, and a lullaby. The built-in timer lets you set 30, 60, or 120-minute intervals, making it ideal for naps and nighttime sleep schedules.

Travel-Ready & Easy to Clean

Its compact size makes it perfect for travel, fitting easily in your diaper bag, backpack, or tote. The machine features a durable, baby-safe clip to secure it on strollers, car seats, or cribs. The food-grade silicone cover is removable, allowing for simple hand washing or dishwasher cleaning.

Cute Shapes & Safe Design

Available in four adorable animal shapes – Fox, Dinosaur, Unicorn, and Penguin – the Pocket Baby Soother adds a touch of fun and comfort to your baby’s sleep space. It has been rigorously tested to meet toy safety standards and is designed to be safe for little hands and curious play.

USB Rechargeable & Long-Lasting Power

Powered by a USB rechargeable battery, the device offers up to 12 hours of continuous use on a single charge. It comes with a convenient white USB cable for easy charging on-the-go. However, for optimal performance, it's recommended to turn it off and unplug every 14 days.

The Must-Have Registry Gift

Whether you're preparing for a new baby or looking to gift a thoughtful sleep aid, the YOGASLEEP Pocket Baby Soother Fox is an ideal choice. It combines practicality, style, and soothing sound in a single compact unit that helps create a calm and comfortable sleep environment for your little one.
