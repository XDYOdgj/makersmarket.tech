EVERLOVE Montessori Baby Crib Mobile with Digital Music Box

A perfect blend of style and sensory development, this baby crib mobile is designed to provide your newborn with visual stimulation and soothing music.

Visual Stimulation for Newborns

This Montessori mobile is thoughtfully crafted to captivate your baby's attention with high contrast black and white patterns. Designed for babies aged 0-5 months, it offers a gentle introduction to visual development through rotating shapes and colors that are ideal for a newborn's evolving eyesight.

Perfect for nurseries and baby rooms, this mobile helps your little one stay engaged while creating a calming atmosphere.

Soothing Digital Music Experience

Bid farewell to wind-up mechanisms! This crib mobile features an upgraded digital music box that plays 12 lullabies with 3 different modes: rotating only, music only, or both. It automatically stops after 30 minutes to help maintain a peaceful sleep environment. Requires 2 AA batteries (not included), and is easy to assemble for quick setup.

Premium Materials for Safety

Constructed from durable and safe ABS plastic, this baby crib mobile is designed to be non-toxic and gentle on your little one. The adjustable arm ensures compatibility with most crib rail widths, up to 1.54 inches, and can be easily installed without the need for screws or tools.

Important Considerations

Please note that this product is not suitable for bassinet use and may not fit all bassinet models. Always check crib compatibility before purchasing to ensure a safe and secure installation.

Give your baby the gift of curiosity and comfort with this stylish, educational, and soothing crib mobile from EverLove.
