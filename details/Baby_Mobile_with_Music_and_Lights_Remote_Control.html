Caterbee Baby Mobile with Music and Lights

A delightful addition to your baby's nursery, the Caterbee Baby Mobile is designed to engage and comfort your little one from birth. With a blend of soothing lights, gentle music, and interactive features, this mobile creates a calm and stimulating environment for your baby's early development.

Remote Control & Timer Setting

Enjoy easy control of the mobile from up to 2.5 feet away using the included remote. The timer function allows you to set the duration of play, making it ideal for naptime and bedtime. This ensures a peaceful and focused environment for your baby.

Projection & Auto Rotation

The built-in projection system casts gentle, soothing images on the ceiling, offering a mesmerizing visual experience for your baby. The auto-rotation feature slowly spins the colorful cartoon toys, keeping your baby entertained and helping to build their visual tracking abilities.

Adjustable Light & Music Box

Enhance your baby's comfort with adjustable lights that create a soft ambiance. The music box features a volume control option, allowing you to customize the sound level for a peaceful sleep environment. Included three AA batteries make it ready to use right out of the box.

Universal Fit & Versatile Design

This mobile is designed to fit a variety of cribs and baby gear, including play yards, travel cribs, car seats, and strollers. With a frame compatibility range of 0.46" to 1.55", it's a versatile accessory that can grow with your baby over time.
