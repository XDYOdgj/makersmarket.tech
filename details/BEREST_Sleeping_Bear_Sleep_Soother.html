BEREST Sleeping Bear - Ultimate Baby Sleep Soother

A soft, huggable companion that mimics the calming sounds of your heart and the gentle glow of a womb, helping your baby drift into a peaceful sleep every night.

Designed to be both a soothing sleep aid and a charming nursery decoration, the BEREST Sleeping Bear is the ideal gift for a baby shower or newborn baby. With its built-in baby cry sensor, the bear automatically senses when your little one stirs and plays a selection of calming sounds to help them settle back to sleep. These include soft white noise, gentle rain, lullaby tunes, fan sounds, and more—proven to ease stress and promote better sleep in babies.

The softly glowing pink night light on the bear's tummy creates a warm and comforting ambiance in the nursery, helping your baby feel safe and secure in the dark. Plus, it's incredibly easy to use—simply activate the sound and light features with the included velcro strap, and let the bear stay by your baby's crib or Moses basket for bedtime. The adjustable volume ensures a personalized experience for your baby, and the compact design makes it perfect for travel.

Lightweight, portable, and easy to clean, the BEREST Sleeping Bear is a must-have for parents seeking a reliable and gentle solution to help their babies sleep better. Its soft fabric and cozy design make it a baby's favorite, while the built-in technology gives you peace of mind when your baby needs it most.

Key Features:
- Mimics the soothing sounds of a mother's heartbeat
- Built-in baby cry sensor that automatically plays calming sounds
- Soft pink night light for a womb-like glow
- 9 different white noise and lullaby options to help soothe and comfort
- Adjustable volume for personalized use
- Easy to use and fully portable with velcro strap
- Perfect for travel or nursery decor
