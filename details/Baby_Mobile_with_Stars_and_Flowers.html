Charming Baby Crib Mobile - Soft Felt Design for Nursery Decor

This delightful baby mobile brings a touch of whimsy to your nursery with its soft felt design featuring bees, flowers, and leaves. It's the perfect addition for any infant's room, offering both style and comfort.

Soothing Movement for Baby's Development

Suspended from a sturdy wooden arched frame, the mobile sways gently to help soothe your little one. Its soft, flowing motion creates a calming environment that's ideal for sleep and relaxation.

Vibrant Colors and Shapes to Spark Curiosity

The mobile features bright yellow and white hues, combined with playful shapes, to captivate your baby's attention. These elements support early visual development and make for an engaging nursery centerpiece.

Versatile and Adaptable Decor Solution

Designed to be hung above cribs, playpens, or changing tables, this mobile adds a beautiful focal point to any nursery setup. Its compact size makes it easy to install and perfect for small spaces.

Product Dimensions

With a diameter of approximately 9 inches, this mobile is compatible with most standard-sized cribs and nursery areas. It’s ideal for creating a serene and stylish atmosphere for your baby.

Note: The crib arm is not included and must be purchased separately. Always keep the mobile out of your baby's reach for safety.
