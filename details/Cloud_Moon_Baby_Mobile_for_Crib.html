Cloud Moon Crib Mobile

Designed with a soft, flowing micro boucle fabric, this crib mobile features a delicate white cloud with a central moon and surrounding stars. The minimalist design is perfect for a neutral boho nursery, offering a calming and stylish touch that works well for both boys and girls.

With a diameter of approximately 23cm and a length of around 60cm from top to bottom, this mobile is ideal for ceiling mounting or attaching to a cot arm that is the correct height. It's also suitable for use inside a canopy bed, adding a gentle motion to your baby's room.

Made from eco-friendly and non-toxic materials, this mobile is designed to engage your baby's senses while supporting their developmental growth. Its soft textures and subtle colors offer a soothing visual experience that enhances the overall nursery ambiance.

This non-toy mobile is perfect for newborns, infants, and new parents looking to create a serene and welcoming space for their little one. The combination of neutral tones, including white, cream, light coffee, and wood, ensures it blends seamlessly with any nursery or home decor style.
