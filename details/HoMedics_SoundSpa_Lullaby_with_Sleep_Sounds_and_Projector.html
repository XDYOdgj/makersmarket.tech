Homedics SoundSpa Lullaby – Soothing Sounds & Calming Projector for Baby & Kids Sleep

Help your little ones drift off to dreamland with the ultimate sleep aid: Homedics SoundSpa Lullaby. This innovative product combines a sound machine with a projector to provide a calming atmosphere for bedtime routines.

6 Soothing Sleep Sounds

Choose from White Noise, Heartbeat, Shushing, Lullaby, Rock-A-Bye, or Twinkle Twinkle to create the perfect sleep environment. The adjustable volume ensures a personalized experience, while the downward-facing speaker enhances sound resonance for deeper relaxation.

Calm Kids with Calming Projector

Project 9 different Bedtime Buddy images onto your wall or ceiling with the soft glow and friendly visuals. The projector tilts for easy adjustment, making it simple to place the images exactly where they're needed to help your child fall asleep effortlessly.

Auto Shut Off Feature

Set the auto-off timer for 30 or 60 minutes to ensure your baby gets a restful night’s sleep. The LED function also includes an auto shut off feature to minimize light exposure and promote better sleep quality.

Travel-Ready Design

Designed for convenience, the Homedics SoundSpa Lullaby is made of durable plastic and is compact enough to fit in any luggage. Take your baby's favorite sleep environment on the go and help them relax anywhere with this portable solution.

What’s in the Box

  • 1 x Homedics SoundSpa Lullaby
  • 1 x AC Adapter
  • 1 x Quick-Start Guide
  • 1 x 4”x4” Frameable Bedtime Buddy Artwork
