Cloud b Ocean Projector Nightlight with 2 White Noises & 2 Soothing Sounds

A calming addition to your baby's room, designed to help create a peaceful sleep environment with adjustable settings and a built-in cry sensor.

TURTLE TRANQUIL: The Eco-Friendly Sleep Soother

Meet the new eco version of the beloved Tranquil Turtle! This upgraded model brings more comfort, sustainability and advanced features with a sleek, rechargeable design. Made from 100% recycled polyester fabric and filling, the Tranquil Turtle - Green is GRS certified, ensuring it's not only good for the planet but also safe for your child.

Soft Sound & Light for Gentle Sleep

The Tranquil Turtle - Green is a charming musical turtle that emits soft, soothing light and creates gentle ocean waves on your ceiling. Choose from two calming underwater sounds or two gentle melodies for a peaceful white noise effect. Perfect for helping babies drift off to sleep with ease.

ADJUSTABLE FOR CUSTOMIZED COMFORT

Customize your sleep environment with adjustable brightness and motion settings. The built-in cry sensor detects sounds and automatically activates the light and sound, providing immediate comfort. With a 23-minute automatic shut-off, it's ideal for use in baby rooms or children's bedrooms for safe, uninterrupted sleep.

SUSTAINABLE CHOICE FOR A GREENER FUTURE

Cloud b is committed to sustainability with FSC certified packaging. This ensures our products come from sustainable forests, supporting environmental conservation and biodiversity. The refillable design makes it an eco-conscious choice for parents who care about the planet and their child's well-being.
