Nuby Support Pod Pillow Cover Dr. Talbot's, Tropical Print

This soft and stylish pillow cover is designed to grow with your baby, making it a versatile companion from birth through 12 months. Ideal for nursing, tummy time, propping, and helping your little one learn to sit up on their own, it’s built for every stage of development.

The removable cover makes cleaning a breeze. Simply take it off and replace it with a fresh one, ensuring your baby stays clean and comfortable. Machine-washable and easy to maintain, it’s perfect for parents who love to keep things tidy.

With a tropical print design, this pillow cover adds a fun and vibrant touch to any nursery. Choose from a wide range of patterns that suit your baby’s gender or keep your space gender-neutral. It’s the perfect way to match your nursery décor with a touch of personality.

Crafted from a blend of 65% polyester and 35% cotton, this cover is ultra-soft and gentle on your baby’s delicate skin. The fabric strip over the zipper ensures that no hard surfaces are exposed, keeping your baby safe and protected at all times.

Designed to fit seamlessly with the Dr. Talbot's Support Pod infant feeding and support pillow, this slipcover is interchangeable. It’s a must-have for any parent looking to create a safe, stylish, and functional nursery environment.

Whether you're preparing for a baby shower or looking to gift a practical, essential item to new parents, the Support Pod Pillow Cover is an ideal choice. It combines comfort, style, and durability in one.
