Nuby Lifelike Animated Sleeping Fox with 8 Soothing Lullabies & 4 Calming White Noises

This adorable motion and sound plush is designed to help babies drift off to sleep with comforting sounds and gentle movement. Featuring 8 popular lullabies from classics like Brahms Lullaby, "Are You Sleeping," and "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star," it offers a peaceful bedtime ritual for your little one.

In addition to the soothing lullabies, the Nuby Sleeping Fox also includes 4 calming white noises such as a heartbeat, gentle snoring, and more. These life-like sounds provide a sense of security, mimicking the comforting presence of a parent nearby.

Each plush is adorned with an adorable heart-shaped mark on the tummy, making it instantly recognizable and endearing. Simply press the heart to activate the sound, breathing motion, or soft light. Another gentle press will turn these features off, allowing for easy control.

Thoughtfully crafted, the Nuby Sleep Soother supports all of baby's senses with a soft touch, subtle breathing motion, and low light. This combination creates a calming environment, helping sensitive little ones relax and fall asleep effortlessly.

With a friendly sleeping face and ultra-cuddlable design, this little fox encourages bedtime with a sense of comfort and safety. Its charming presence can be a reassuring companion for your baby in the nursery.

The intuitive control panel makes it simple for parents to customize the experience. Options include selecting lullabies, adjusting white noise, turning the tummy light on or off, and setting the volume to suit your baby's preferences.

To conserve battery life, the Motion and Sound Plush includes an automatic shut-off function. After 30 minutes of play, the sounds will stop. The soft light turns off after 15 minutes, and the breathing motion stops after 10 minutes, ensuring energy efficiency and long-lasting use.
