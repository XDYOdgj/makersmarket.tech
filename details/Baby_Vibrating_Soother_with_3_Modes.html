CHICWAY Baby Vibrating Soother for Peaceful Sleep

Soothe your little one into dreamland with the gentle touch of CHICWAY’s vibrating baby soother. Designed to mimic the comforting rhythm of a parent’s embrace, this modern crib sleep aid helps babies relax and fall asleep faster.

Gentle Vibrations for Better Sleep

Our baby sleep soother offers soothing vibrations that gently rock your baby, helping them drift off into a restful night. The soft, consistent motion mimics the calming effect of a parent’s touch, making bedtime easier for both you and your little one.

3 Adjustable Vibration Modes

Every baby is different, so we’ve added 3 customizable vibration modes to suit your baby’s unique needs. Choose from a soft hum, a rhythmic pulse, or a gentle sway to create the ideal sleep environment for your baby.

60-Min Auto-Off Timer

The baby crib soother includes a 60-minute progressive timer that gradually reduces vibration intensity, ensuring your baby stays asleep without sudden interruptions. It's perfect for late-night awakenings and offers consistent support for better sleep habits.

Travel-Ready Design

Designed for on-the-go families, this portable crib vibrator is easy to carry and perfect for road trips, vacations, or moving between rooms. The Type-C fast charging feature ensures it’s always ready when you need it, with up to 3 hours of battery life.

Easy to Clean & Maintain

Made from soft cotton, this baby sleeping accessory is effortless to clean. Simply wipe with a damp cloth and let it air dry. No bleach or ironing required, keeping it safe and gentle for your baby’s delicate skin.

Universal Fit for All Cribs and Bassinets

With a versatile design, our vibrating pad fits most standard cribs and bassinets. It's an ideal baby crib soother for newborns and a helpful crib accessory for parents seeking to ensure every night is calm and restful.

A Must-Have for Newborns and Parents

Whether you're at home or traveling, the CHICWAY baby soother is a perfect addition to your baby’s bedtime routine. It's not just a gift for a baby shower or registry—this baby sleep aid is a lifeline for tired parents and a comfort for little ones everywhere.
