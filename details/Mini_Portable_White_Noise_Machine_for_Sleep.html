BodyHush Mini Portable Sound White Noise Machine

A compact yet powerful sleep aid designed to help babies, kids, and adults relax and sleep better. With 30 soothing sounds, 32 volume levels, and an auto-off timer, this mini sound machine is the perfect companion for home, travel, or office use.

Compact & Travel-Friendly

This small sound machine is lightweight and easy to carry, fitting perfectly in your travel bag, backpack, or even a pocket. Its portable design makes it ideal for use anywhere, from your bedroom to a hotel room.

Powerful Sound Masking

With 32 volume levels, this sleep soother delivers enough sound to effectively block out distractions and create a peaceful environment. Whether it's for your baby's sleep, office privacy, or calming pets, this machine ensures a restful ambiance.

30 Unique Non-Looping Sounds

Choose from a variety of natural sounds like rain, waves, lullabies, birds, and more. These non-looping options help you fall asleep faster or stay relaxed during meditation, offering a different auditory experience every time you use it.

Long-Lasting Battery

Equipped with a long battery life, this white noise machine offers up to 22 hours of continuous use on a single charge. It can also be used via a power outlet for uninterrupted performance.

Smart Timer & Memory Function

Customize your sleep time with the timer function that allows 30, 60, or 90 minutes of continuous play. Plus, the memory function keeps your custom settings, so you never lose your preferred sound or volume.

Durable & Safe

Certified by FCC, CE, and RoHS, this machine is built for long-term use and safety. The 18-month warranty ensures peace of mind, and our dedicated customer support is always ready to assist you with any questions.

Whether you're looking for a sleep soother for your little one, a calming device for yourself, or something to help your pets sleep better, the BodyHush Mini Portable Sound White Noise Machine is the perfect solution. Experience the peace of natural sounds anytime, anywhere.
