Baby Shusher

Portable Sound Machine - Mom-Like 'Shh!' Sound for Baby Sleep

Give your baby the gentle, comforting sounds they need to sleep peacefully and stay calm. The Baby Shusher is designed to mimic the soothing rhythm of a mother’s voice, helping to break crying spells and promote restful sleep for your little one.

Perfect for newborns, toddlers, and even babies in NICUs, this sound machine is a trusted companion for parents seeking calm and consistency. Unlike traditional white noise machines, it offers real human 'Shh!' sounds that mimic the lullabies of a parent, making it a natural choice for baby sleep training and bedtime routines.

  • Simple, No-Tech Design: No complicated apps or Wi-Fi connections — just intuitive controls for volume and timer. Easy to use and ideal for everyday sleep support.
  • Travel-Ready: Compact, lightweight, and portable, the Baby Shusher comes with 2 AA batteries and a convenient strap for strollers, bags, or cribs. Keep your baby calm on the go with this essential travel accessory.
  • Pediatrician-Approved: Designed with baby comfort in mind, Baby Shusher has been used in NICUs and awarded "Mom's Choice." Over 3 million babies have been soothed by its natural, rhythmic sounds worldwide.

Whether you're preparing for a baby shower, adding to your registry, or looking for a gift that brings comfort, the Baby Shusher is a must-have. It complements any baby girl or baby boy essentials list and delivers a calming experience every time.

Create a Calm Environment for Your Baby

With no lights, no music, and no distractions — just the soft, familiar sounds of a parent’s lullaby — Baby Shusher is more than a sleep soother. It’s a tool for creating a nurturing atmosphere that supports healthy sleep habits and emotional well-being.
