ZPECC Toddler Pillow with Pillowcase - 13x18 Organic Cotton Baby Essential

Give your little one the comfort they deserve with this soft, hypoallergenic toddler pillow. Made from 100% organic cotton, it's perfect for creating a safe and cozy sleep environment, whether at home or on the go.

Premium Organic Fabric

This pillow is certified with both Organic Content Standard 100 and OEKO-TEX Standard 100, ensuring it's free from harmful chemicals and safe for your baby's sensitive skin. The breathable and skin-friendly material is designed to keep your child comfortable throughout the day or night.

Perfect for Every Sleep Setting

Designed with a 13x18 size, this toddler pillow fits perfectly in cribs, toddler beds, strollers, and other baby essentials. It’s also ideal for use in daycare centers, classrooms, or during travel and camping trips to provide consistent support and softness wherever your little one goes.

Easy to Maintain

Machine washable and tumble dryable, this pillow is built to last. It won’t shrink, pill, or clump, making it a reliable choice for parents who want ease and durability in every product. Simply air dry it in the sun to keep it flat and fresh.

Travel-Friendly Comfort

Whether you're heading to the park, on a camping trip, or just out for a quick errand, this pillow is a must-have. Its lightweight and compact design makes it easy to carry, while the soft microfiber filling ensures a restful and safe sleep experience for your child.

Thoughtful Design for Growing Kids

Designed with toddlers aged 2 and up in mind, this pillow is gentle on developing bodies and supports healthy sleep habits. The grey stripe design adds a modern and stylish touch to nursery décor, making it both functional and fashionable.
